<p><p>Flight conveyors are a type of material handling system used widely in various industries. There are two main types of flight conveyors: Horizontal Type and Inclined Type. </p><p>Horizontal Type flight conveyors are designed to move materials horizontally from one point to another. They have a flat conveying surface and are typically used for short-distance transportation within a facility.</p><p>Inclined Type flight conveyors, on the other hand, are designed to transport materials at an incline. They are equipped with inclined flights or paddles to effectively move materials uphill or downhill, making them ideal for applications where elevation changes are required.</p></p>
