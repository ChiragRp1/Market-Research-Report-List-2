<p><p>Barrier resins are used in packaging materials to provide protection against oxygen, moisture, and other contaminants. EVOH resin, or ethylene vinyl alcohol, offers excellent barrier properties and is commonly used in food packaging. PVDC, or polyvinylidene chloride, is another widely used barrier resin that provides high moisture and oxygen resistance. PEN, or polyethylene naphthalate, is a barrier resin known for its excellent gas barrier properties and resistance to chemicals and heat. Other types of barrier resins may include polyesters, polyamides, and polypropylene, each offering unique barrier properties and suitability for different applications.</p></p>
