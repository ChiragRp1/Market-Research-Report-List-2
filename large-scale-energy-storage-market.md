<p><p>Large-scale energy storage refers to the ability to store a significant amount of energy on a large scale, typically for use during peak demand periods or when renewable energy sources are not generating power. Large-scale energy storage can efficiently integrate renewable energy into the grid and mitigate the challenges associated with intermittency.</p><p>The market is also witnessing innovative developments and advancements in energy storage technologies.
