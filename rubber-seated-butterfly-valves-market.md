<p><p>Rubber seated butterfly valves are widely used in various industries for regulating fluid flow. The market is divided into two types: wafer type and lug type. Wafer type valves are compact, lightweight, and require less installation space. They are placed between two flanges and held in place with bolts. On the other hand, lug type valves have threaded inserts on both sides, allowing them to be installed and removed without disconnecting the pipe ends. Both types offer easy installation, maintenance, and reliable sealing capabilities.</p></p>
