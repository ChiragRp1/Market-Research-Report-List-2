<p><p>Neonatal ventilators are medical devices used to assist breathing in newborn infants who require respiratory support. There are two main types of neonatal ventilators: intensive care ventilators and portable/transportable ventilators. Intensive care ventilators are typically used in neonatal intensive care units and provide advanced respiratory support for critically ill newborns. Portable/transportable ventilators, on the other hand, are designed for use during transportation of newborns between hospitals or for home care. These devices are smaller and more lightweight, allowing for easier mobility while ensuring respiratory support for newborns in need.</p></p>
