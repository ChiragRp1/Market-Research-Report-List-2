<p><p>The IR flame detector market includes three main types: Single IR, Dual IR, and Multi IR detectors. Single IR detectors use a single infrared sensor to detect the presence of flames, offering basic flame detection capabilities. Dual IR detectors utilize two infrared sensors to enhance flame detection accuracy, as they can differentiate between flames and other sources of heat. Multi IR detectors employ multiple infrared sensors and advanced algorithms to provide highly reliable flame detection, minimizing false alarms and improving overall performance. These different types cater to varying requirements and ensure effective flame monitoring in different environments.</p></p>
