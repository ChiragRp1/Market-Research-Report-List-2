<p><p>The powered wheelchair market includes two main types: foldable and unfoldable powered wheelchairs. Foldable powered wheelchairs are designed to be easily collapsible, making them convenient for transportation and storage. In contrast, unfoldable powered wheelchairs are not collapsible and are typically sturdier and more stable. These wheelchairs are often preferred by individuals who require a higher weight capacity or need enhanced support and durability. Both types cater to the diverse needs of individuals with mobility issues, providing them with greater independence and mobility.</p></p>
