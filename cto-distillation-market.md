<p><p>CTO distillation refers to a process where crude tall oil (CTO) is separated into different market types for various applications. Tall Oil Rosin (TOR) is used in adhesives and several types of coatings. Tall Oil Pitch (TOP) is utilized as a binder in carbon black production. Distilled Tall Oil (DTO) is commonly used in the production of biodiesel, lubricants, and cleaners. Lastly, Tall Oil Fatty Acids (TOFA) find their applications in soaps, detergents, and other chemical formulations. These market types are derived from the same CTO distillation process but serve different purposes in various industries.</p></p>
