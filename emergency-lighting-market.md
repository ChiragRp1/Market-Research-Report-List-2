<p><ul><li>Self-contained Power Supply</li><li>Centralized Power Supply</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The emergency lighting market comprises two major types: self-contained power supply and centralized power supply. Self-contained power supply systems are standalone units that consist of a battery, charging system, and lighting fixtures. These systems are mainly used in small buildings or areas where power outage is infrequent. On the other hand, centralized power supply systems connect multiple lighting units to a central battery system. This type is commonly employed in large buildings or areas where a more comprehensive emergency lighting solution is required.</p></p>
