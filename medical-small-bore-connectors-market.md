<p><p>The medical small-bore connectors market includes the types of liquid bore connectors and gas bore connectors. Liquid bore connectors are used for connecting devices that carry liquids, such as syringes or IV lines. They ensure safe and secure connections, preventing leaks that could lead to infections or incorrect medication delivery. Gas bore connectors, on the other hand, are used for connecting devices that carry gases, such as oxygen or anesthesia. They play a critical role in maintaining a controlled flow of gases and preventing accidental disconnections that could be dangerous for patients.</p></p>
