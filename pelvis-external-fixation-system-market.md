<p><p>The Pelvis External Fixation System market consists of two types: Tubular and Circular. The Tubular system involves the use of a tubular frame attached to the pelvic bones externally, providing stabilization and support during the healing process. On the other hand, the Circular system utilizes a circular frame around the pelvis, enabling adjustable fixation and control over the bone fragments. Both systems offer effective solutions for pelvis fractures, providing stability and aiding in successful recovery.</p></p>
