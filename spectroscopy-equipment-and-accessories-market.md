<p><p>The spectroscopy equipment and accessories market can be categorized into three major types: molecular spectrometers, atomic spectrometers, and mass spectrometers. Molecular spectrometers are used to analyze the absorption, emission, and scattering of light by molecules, allowing researchers to identify and quantify various compounds. Atomic spectrometers analyze the electromagnetic radiation emitted by atoms to determine elemental compositions. Mass spectrometers measure the mass-to-charge ratio of ions, helping in the identification of compounds based on their molecular masses. These instruments find applications in various scientific fields, including chemistry, biology, and pharmacology.</p></p>
