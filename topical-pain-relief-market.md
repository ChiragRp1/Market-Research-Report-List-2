<p><p>The topical pain relief market consists of two main types: non-opioids and opioids. Non-opioids include medications like nonsteroidal anti-inflammatory drugs (NSAIDs) and local anesthetics, which relieve pain by reducing inflammation or numbing the area. These types of medications are commonly used for mild to moderate pain relief. On the other hand, opioids, such as codeine or morphine, are strong analgesics that work by binding to opioid receptors in the body. They are typically prescribed for severe pain but come with a higher risk of addiction and other side effects.</p></p>
