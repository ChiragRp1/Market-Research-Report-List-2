Crystalline silica powder is composed of tiny particles with a crystalline structure. Fused silica powder is produced by melting silica and then cooling it rapidly to form amorphous particles. Spherical silica powder is spherical in shape and offers improved flowability and dispersion properties. These different types of silica fillers cater to the specific needs of the electronics industry, providing various benefits and applications.</p></p>
