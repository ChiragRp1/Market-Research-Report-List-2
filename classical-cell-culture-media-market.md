<p><p>Classical cell culture media market types can be categorized into three main groups. The first is Minimum Essential Medium (MEM) type, which provides the basic nutrient requirements for cell growth. The second is Modification type, where specific components are added or removed from MEM to suit the particular needs of different cell types. The last category is Others, which includes specialized media formulations such as serum-free media, chemically defined media, and specialty media designed for specific cell culture applications. These different types cater to the diverse requirements of cell culture research and applications.</p></p>
