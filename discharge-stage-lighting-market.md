<p><p>Discharge stage lighting refers to the type of lighting used in stage productions and concerts. There are three main types of discharge lamps in the market. Low-pressure gas discharge lamps are energy-efficient and emit a soft, warm light. High-pressure discharge lamps produce a brighter, whiter light and are commonly used in outdoor events. Super high-pressure gas discharge lamps offer the highest luminosity and are suitable for large venues or stadiums. These lamps are favored for their long lifespan and ability to produce intense, colorful lighting effects on stage.</p></p>
