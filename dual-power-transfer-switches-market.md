<p><p>A Dual Power Transfer Switch (DPTS) is a device that ensures seamless power supply during electrical power interruptions. It allows for the automatic transfer of electrical load between two sources, typically between a utility power source and an alternate power source such as a generator. <p><p>Dual power transfer switches are devices used to transfer power from one source to another, ensuring uninterrupted electricity supply during power outages or when switching between different power sources. These switches are available in two market types: automatic and manual. Automatic transfer switches are designed to automatically detect power loss and transfer the load to an alternate source without human intervention. On the other hand, manual transfer switches require manual operation to switch the power source. Both types serve the purpose of ensuring consistent power supply, but differ in their operation and level of automation.</p></p>
