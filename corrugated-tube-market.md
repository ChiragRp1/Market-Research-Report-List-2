<p><ul><li>Single Wall Corrugated (SWC)</li><li>Double Wall Corrugated (DWC)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The corrugated tube market is segmented into two main types: single wall corrugated (SWC) and double wall corrugated (DWC). SWC tubes are made of a single layer of corrugated material, offering flexibility and lightweight properties. They are commonly used for electrical wiring and cable protection. On the other hand, DWC tubes consist of two layers, with an inner smooth wall and an outer corrugated wall. DWC tubes provide stronger protection, resistance to external forces, and are often used for applications requiring high durability, such as drainage systems.</p></p>
