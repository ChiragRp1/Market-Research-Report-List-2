<p><ul><li>Circulated Air Incubators</li><li>Still Air Incubators</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Reptile incubator market comprises two main types: circulated air incubators and still air incubators. Circulated air incubators use fans or blowers to evenly distribute warm air throughout the incubator, ensuring a consistent temperature and humidity level. They provide better temperature control and are suitable for most reptile eggs. On the other hand, still air incubators rely on natural convection to regulate temperature but may have localized temperature variations. They are generally less expensive but require extra attention to maintain proper temperature and humidity levels.</p></p>
