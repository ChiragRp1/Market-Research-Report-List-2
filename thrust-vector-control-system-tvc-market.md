<p><p>The Thrust Vector Control System (TVC) market encompasses various types, including the Thrust Vector Actuation System, Thrust Vector Injection System, and Thrust Vector Thruster System. The Thrust Vector Actuation System utilizes actuators to redirect exhaust gas flow, thereby altering the direction of thrust. In contrast, the Thrust Vector Injection System involves injecting fluid into the exhaust stream to generate thrust vectoring. Lastly, the Thrust Vector Thruster System employs dedicated thrusters to control the direction of thrust. These systems play a crucial role in enhancing maneuverability and performance in aerospace applications.</p></p>
