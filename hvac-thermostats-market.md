<p><p>The HVAC thermostat market consists of three main types: plug-in type, immersion type, and surface type thermostats. Plug-in type thermostats are designed to be easily plugged into an electrical outlet and control the heating or cooling system. Immersion type thermostats are specifically designed for controlling the temperature of liquids, such as water heaters. Surface type thermostats are used for controlling the temperature of surfaces, such as heated floors or radiators. These three types cater to different needs and environments, providing efficient temperature control solutions.</p></p>
