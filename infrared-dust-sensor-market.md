<p><p>The Infrared Dust Sensor market includes two types: UART Output and PWM Output. UART Output refers to a sensor that uses a Universal Asynchronous Receiver Transmitter to communicate with other devices. It simplifies data transfer and provides a direct connection to microcontrollers. On the other hand, PWM Output utilizes Pulse Width Modulation to vary the signal's duty cycle. This type of sensor is widely used in applications where precise control of signals is required. Both types cater to different market needs, offering diverse communication options for effective integration with various systems.</p></p>
