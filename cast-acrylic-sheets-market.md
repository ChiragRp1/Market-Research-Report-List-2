<p><strong>The Cast Acrylic Sheets Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cell Cast Acrylic Sheet</li><li>Continuous Cast Acrylic Sheet</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Cast Acrylic Sheets market consists of two types: Cell Cast Acrylic Sheet and Continuous Cast Acrylic Sheet. Cell Cast Acrylic Sheets are produced through a process where liquid acrylic is poured into a mold and then cured, resulting in sheets with excellent clarity and a higher molecular weight. Continuous Cast Acrylic Sheets are made using a continuous casting method, where a film of acrylic is continuously extruded and then cooled, offering high surface quality and dimensional stability. Both types find applications in various industries like automotive, construction, and signage.</p></p>
