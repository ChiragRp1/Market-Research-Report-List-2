<p><p>The lithium battery electrolyte additives market is segmented into various types based on their functionalities. Film-forming additives are substances that form a protective layer on the electrode surface, enhancing battery performance. Conductive additives improve the electrical conductivity of the electrolyte, leading to better overall battery efficiency. Flame retardant additives increase the safety of lithium batteries by preventing or delaying fire initiation. Overcharge protection additives help prevent damage caused by excessive charging. Lastly, the "Other" category includes additives that serve unique purposes or have multiple functionalities, which can range from improving battery life to enhancing thermal stability.</p></p>
