<p><p>The polishing compounds market includes both solid and liquid types. Solid polishing compounds are in a solid form and are typically used on a buffing wheel by rubbing the compound against the surface to be polished. They are effective in removing scratches and restoring the shine of metals. On the other hand, liquid polishing compounds are in liquid form and are applied directly onto the surface to be polished. They are commonly used for polishing delicate objects or areas that are hard to reach.</p></p>
