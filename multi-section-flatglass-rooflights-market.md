<p><p>Multi-section flat glass rooflights are a type of rooflight that includes multiple sections or panels of glass. There are two main types of multi-section flat glass rooflights based on the number of layers of glass used in their construction: double-layered glass and triple-layered glass. Double-layered glass rooflights consist of two layers of glass separated by an air gap, providing enhanced insulation and soundproofing properties. Triple-layered glass rooflights have three layers of glass with two air gaps, further enhancing their insulation capabilities and providing improved thermal efficiency.</p></p>
