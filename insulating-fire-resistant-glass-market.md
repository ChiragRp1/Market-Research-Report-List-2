<p><p>Insulating Fire Resistant Glass is available in various market types. </p><p>1. Sandwich Composite: This type of glass consists of two or more glass panes with an interlayer of fire-resistant material, providing enhanced insulation and fire resistance.</p><p>2. Wire Fire: Wire fire glass contains a wire mesh embedded within the glass, which enhances its structural integrity during a fire and prevents it from shattering or falling apart.</p><p>3. Special Fire: Special fire glass is designed with additional fire-resistant additives or coatings, providing higher resistance against flames, heat, and smoke infiltration.</p><p>4. Hollow Fire: Hollow fire glass incorporates a hollow space within the glass unit, enhancing its thermal insulation properties and offering increased fire resistance.</p></p>
