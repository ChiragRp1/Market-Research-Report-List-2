<p><p>The passive optical components market comprises various types of equipment used in optical communication networks. Optical cables are responsible for transmitting data as light signals. Optical power splitters split the optical signal into multiple outputs. Optical couplers combine or divide optical signals. Optical encoders convert electrical signals into optical ones. Optical connectors provide a means to connect optical fibers. Patch cords and pigtails are used to connect optical components. Optical amplifiers amplify optical signals to maintain signal strength over long distances.</p></p>
