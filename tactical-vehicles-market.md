<p><p>Tactical vehicles are categorized into three types based on their size and capabilities. Light tactical vehicles are small and agile, designed for quick transportation and reconnaissance missions. They offer high mobility and maneuverability in challenging terrains. Medium tactical vehicles are slightly larger, capable of carrying troops, equipment, and supplies over longer distances. They strike a balance between mobility and load-carrying capacity. Heavy tactical vehicles are the largest and most robust, specifically built for transporting heavy armor, artillery, or large quantities of supplies. They provide the highest load-carrying capacity but are less agile.</p></p>
