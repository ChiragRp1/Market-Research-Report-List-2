<p><p>Lasik eye surgery devices market includes two main types of laser technology: Excimer Laser and Femtosecond Laser. Excimer Laser is a high-energy ultraviolet laser that is used to reshape the cornea during the surgery, correcting refractive errors such as nearsightedness, farsightedness, and astigmatism. On the other hand, Femtosecond Laser is a newer technology that uses ultrafast pulses to create a precise and predictable corneal flap, allowing for a more customizable and accurate surgery. Both types of lasers are integral to the success of Lasik eye surgeries.</p></p>
