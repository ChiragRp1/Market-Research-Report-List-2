<p><p>Laser marking is a technology used to engrave, trace, or label materials using lasers. There are various types of lasers used in this market. Fiber lasers utilize optical fibers to amplify and deliver laser beams, making them highly efficient and versatile. Diode lasers generate laser beams through diode bars, offering compact and low-cost options. Solid-state lasers utilize solid materials like crystals as the medium to produce laser beams. CO2 lasers use carbon dioxide gas as the medium and are often employed for marking non-metallic materials.</p></p>
