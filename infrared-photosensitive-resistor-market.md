<p><p>The infrared photosensitive resistor market consists of four types based on the materials used: cadmium sulfide, thallium sulfide, cadmium selenide, and lead sulfide. Cadmium sulfide material is widely used due to its reliable performance and availability. Thallium sulfide material offers high sensitivity in a small size and is suitable for low-power circuits. Cadmium selenide material provides good sensitivity to long wavelengths but is less common. Lead sulfide material has high sensitivity to long wavelengths but is limited in its availability.</p></p>
