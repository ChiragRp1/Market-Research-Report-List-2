<p><p>Radiation monitoring and safety market involves various types of detectors used to measure and protect against radiation exposure. Gas filled detectors, such as Geiger-Muller counters, detect radiation by the ionization of gas molecules. Scintillators work by detecting the light produced when radiation interacts with specific materials. Semiconductor-based detectors use semiconducting materials to generate electrical signals when exposed to radiation. Other types of detectors include thermoluminescent dosimeters and solid-state detectors. Each type serves a specific purpose in monitoring radiation levels, ensuring safety in various industries like healthcare, nuclear power, and environmental monitoring.</p></p>
