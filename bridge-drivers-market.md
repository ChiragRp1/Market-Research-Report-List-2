<p><ul><li>Half Bridge Drivers</li><li>Full Bridge Drivers</li><li>Three-Phase Drivers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Bridge drivers are electronic devices used to control and drive power semiconductor switches in different electronic systems. Half bridge drivers are used in applications where only one switch needs to be controlled, while full bridge drivers are used when both switches need to be controlled independently. Three-phase drivers are used in three-phase systems to control and drive power switches for efficient power distribution. All three types of bridge drivers are essential components in various electronic systems, enabling precise and reliable control of power switch operations.</p></p>
