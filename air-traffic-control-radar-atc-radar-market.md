<p><strong>The Air Traffic Control Radar (ATC-Radar) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Primary Surveillance Radar</li><li>Secondary Surveillance Radar</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Air Traffic Control Radar (ATC-Radar) market consists of two main types of radars, namely Primary Surveillance Radar (PSR) and Secondary Surveillance Radar (SSR). PSR relies on the transmission and reception of radio waves to detect and track aircraft. It provides basic information such as position and velocity. On the other hand, SSR works in conjunction with PSR but also uses transponders onboard aircraft to obtain additional information, including identification, altitude, and flight parameters. This results in a more comprehensive and accurate tracking system for air traffic control purposes.</p></p>
