<p><p>The indoor advertising player market can be categorized into two main types: floor-standing and wall-mounted. Floor-standing players are free-standing display units that are placed on the floor, allowing for easy movement and flexibility in positioning. They are well-suited for larger spaces and areas with high foot traffic. On the other hand, wall-mounted players are fixed to walls, offering a more permanent and space-saving solution. They are commonly used in areas with limited floor space, such as corridors or small rooms.</p></p>
