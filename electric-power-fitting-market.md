<p><ul><li>Suspension Clamp</li><li>Strain Clamp</li><li>Link fitting</li><li>Protective fitting</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electric power fitting market types include suspension clamp, strain clamp, link fitting, protective fitting, and others. Suspension clamps are used to hang conductors on overhead lines and provide mechanical support. Strain clamps are used to fix conductors and provide electrical continuity without damaging them. Link fittings connect different conductors or accessories in a power system. Protective fittings protect conductors from environmental factors such as weather and vandalism. Other types include fittings for insulation and grounding purposes. These fittings are essential for ensuring safe and reliable electricity transmission and distribution.</p></p>
