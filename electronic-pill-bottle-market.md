<p><p>The electronic pill bottle market consists of two main types: "Connect to Phone" and "Operate Independently". Users can receive reminder notifications, track medication intake, and access dosage instructions through mobile apps. On the other hand, "Operate Independently" bottles function autonomously, offering built-in alarms and timers to remind users when to take their medication. These bottles do not require a smartphone connection but provide similar functionalities to ensure medication adherence.</p></p>
