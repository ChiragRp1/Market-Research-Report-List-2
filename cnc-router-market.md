<p><p>A CNC router, or Computer Numerical Control router, is a machine that uses computer programming to control the movement of a cutting tool. These machines enable individuals and small businesses to create intricate designs and prototypes with ease.</p><p>The current market for CNC routers is also experiencing growth, with manufacturers constantly innovating to enhance machine capabilities.
