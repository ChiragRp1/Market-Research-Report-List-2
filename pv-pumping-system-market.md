<p><ul><li>Submersible</li><li>Surface Pumps</li></ul></p>
<p><p>PV pumping systems are used for water supply and irrigation in remote areas, where access to electricity is limited. Submersible pumps are designed to be underwater and are suitable for deep wells. They are efficient and reliable, providing a consistent water supply. Surface pumps, on the other hand, are placed above ground level and are suitable for shallow wells or surface water sources. They are cost-effective and easier to maintain. Both types of pumps utilize solar energy to power the pumping process, making them environmentally friendly and sustainable.</p></p>
