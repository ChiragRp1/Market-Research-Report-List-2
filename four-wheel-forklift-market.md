<p><p>A four wheel forklift, also known as a counterbalance forklift, is a type of material handling equipment used in warehouses, distribution centers, and manufacturing facilities for lifting and transporting heavy loads. It is designed with a counterbalance weight at the rear to offset the load being lifted, eliminating the need for additional support. Four wheel forklifts are versatile, capable of maneuvering in tight spaces and handling various types of loads, making them popular in a wide range of industries.</p><p>The global four wheel forklift market is expected to experience significant growth in the coming years.
