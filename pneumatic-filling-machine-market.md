<p><p>Pneumatic filling machines are categorized into three market types based on their operation: automatic, semi-automatic, and manual. </p><p>Automatic pneumatic filling machines are fully automated, requiring minimal human intervention. They can continuously fill and seal products without the need for operator control. </p><p>Semi-automatic machines require some manual intervention, such as loading products or activating the filling process, but most of the operations are automated. They offer a balance between automation and manual control. </p><p>Manual pneumatic filling machines rely entirely on human operators to perform filling, sealing, and other necessary tasks. These machines are suitable for small-scale production or specific applications requiring precision and control.</p></p>
