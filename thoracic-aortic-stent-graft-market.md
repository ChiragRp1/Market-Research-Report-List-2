<p><ul><li>Metal</li><li>Polymer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Thoracic Aortic Stent Graft market involves two main types: Metal and Polymer stents. Metal stent grafts are made of durable materials such as stainless steel or nitinol, providing strong structural support to reinforce the weakened aortic walls. On the other hand, Polymer stent grafts are made of flexible materials like polyester or ePTFE, offering greater conformability to the natural shape of the aorta. Both types have their advantages and are used in treating thoracic aortic aneurysms, choosing the appropriate stent graft depends on the patient's specific needs and the surgeon's preference.</p></p>
