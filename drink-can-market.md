<p><p>The drink can market consists of two main types: two-pieces can and three-pieces can. Two-pieces cans are constructed using a single piece of metal for the body and the top lid, which is attached through a process known as bonding. On the other hand, three-pieces cans are made by joining three separate components - the body, top lid, and bottom lid - using a seaming process. Both types of cans are commonly used in the beverage industry for packaging various drinks, providing durability and convenience for consumers.</p></p>
