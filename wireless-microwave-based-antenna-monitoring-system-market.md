<p><p>The wireless microwave-based antenna monitoring system market includes two main types: analog microwave wireless monitoring systems and digital microwave wireless monitoring systems. Analog systems utilize continuous wave signals for monitoring antenna performance, providing real-time data on signal strength and quality. On the other hand, digital systems utilize advanced digital signal processing techniques to analyze antenna performance and provide more detailed information, such as modulation accuracy and error rates. Both types offer effective means of monitoring and managing antennas, with digital systems offering more advanced features for comprehensive analysis.</p></p>
