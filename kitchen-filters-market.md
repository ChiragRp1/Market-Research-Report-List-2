<p><strong>The Kitchen Filters Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Duct Filters</li><li>Non-Duct Filters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Kitchen filters market is divided into two main types: duct filters and non-duct filters. Duct filters are installed directly in the kitchen exhaust system and collect grease and other airborne particles as the air passes through them. They require regular maintenance and cleaning to ensure proper functionality. Non-duct filters, on the other hand, do not require a direct connection to the exhaust system and rely on recirculating the air. They typically use activated charcoal or other technologies to remove odors and pollutants from the air. Both types cater to different needs and preferences in maintaining a clean and healthy kitchen environment.</p></p>
