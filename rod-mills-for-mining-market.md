<p><p>Rod mills are commonly used in the mining industry to grind materials into fine particles. There are two main types: wet grinding type and dry grinding type. In wet grinding type, materials are fed through the water-filled mill barrel while being crushed and ground. This type is suitable for materials with high moisture content or when a slurry product is required. In dry grinding type, materials are fed into the mill barrel without water and undergo crushing and grinding processes. This type is suitable for materials with low moisture content and produces a dry product.</p></p>
