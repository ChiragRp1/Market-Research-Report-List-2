<p><p>DC Gearmotors are motors that combine a DC motor and a gear mechanism to provide high torque output and controlled speed. DC Right Angle Gearmotors are designed with the output shaft perpendicular to the motor shaft, suitable for applications where space is limited and 90-degree motion is required. DC Parallel Shaft Gearmotors have the output shaft parallel to the motor shaft, making them ideal for applications that require high torque and low speed. Both types cater to different needs and are extensively used in various industries for their respective advantages.</p></p>
