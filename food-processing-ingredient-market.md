<p><p>The food processing ingredient market consists of various types such as modified starch & starch derivatives, protein, food stabilizers, emulsifiers, yeast, enzymes, acidity regulators, antioxidants, and release agents. Food stabilizers and emulsifiers help in maintaining texture and stability. Yeast and enzymes are utilized for fermentation and improving food processing efficiency. Acidity regulators are added to adjust pH levels, whereas antioxidants prevent food spoilage. Lastly, release agents are used to prevent food from sticking to surfaces during processing and packaging.</p></p>
