<p><p>UHPLC columns are used in liquid chromatography to separate and analyze complex mixtures. Packed columns consist of a stationary phase packed into a column, allowing for higher sample capacity but slower analysis times. Capillary columns, on the other hand, have a smaller inner diameter, resulting in faster analysis times but lower sample capacity. Both types of columns offer high efficiency and resolution for the separation of compounds in pharmaceutical, environmental, and food industries, leading to improved detection and quantification of analytes.</p></p>
