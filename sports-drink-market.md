<p><strong>The Sports Drink Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hypotonic Sports Drink</li><li>Isotonic Sports Drink</li><li>Hypertonic Sports Drink</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Sports drinks are categorized into three types based on their compositions and effects on the body. Hypotonic sports drinks have a lower concentration of carbohydrates and electrolytes, making them ideal for quick hydration without providing substantial energy. Isotonic sports drinks have a balanced concentration of fluids, carbohydrates, and electrolytes, providing hydration and energy during medium-intensity workouts. Hypertonic sports drinks have a higher concentration of carbohydrates, suitable for athletes needing sustained energy and nutrient replenishment during prolonged, high-intensity activities. These market segments cater to athletes with varying hydration and energy requirements.</p></p>
