<p><ul><li>Mono</li><li>Stereo</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The camera microphone market includes two main types: mono and stereo microphones. Mono microphones are designed to capture audio in a single channel, making them suitable for basic recordings. On the other hand, stereo microphones have two channels that provide a more immersive sound experience, capturing audio from different directions and creating a sense of depth. They are ideal for professional recordings, videography, and filmmaking purposes. These microphone types offer different options for capturing and reproducing audio, catering to varying needs and preferences in the camera industry.</p></p>
