<p><p>Power connectors are classified into three main types: light-duty, medium-duty, and heavy-duty, based on their intended applications and load-bearing capacities. Light-duty connectors are designed for small-scale applications with low power requirements, such as consumer electronics or personal computer peripherals. Medium-duty connectors are capable of handling more power, making them suitable for applications like appliances or industrial machinery. On the other hand, heavy-duty connectors are specifically designed for high-power applications, like electric vehicles or heavy machinery, providing a robust and reliable connection to carry substantial electrical currents.</p></p>
