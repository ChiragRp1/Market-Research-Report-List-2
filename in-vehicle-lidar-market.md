<p><p>The in-vehicle LiDAR market includes two main types: medium range and long range. Medium range LiDAR systems typically have a detection range of around 30-100 meters, making them suitable for advanced driver-assistance systems (ADAS) applications such as lane keeping and collision avoidance. On the other hand, long range LiDAR systems offer a detection range of over 100 meters, enabling more complex autonomous driving functions like highway driving and autonomous parking. Both types play a crucial role in enhancing vehicle safety and enabling autonomous driving capabilities.</p></p>
