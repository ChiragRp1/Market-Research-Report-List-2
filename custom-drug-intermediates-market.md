<p><p>The Custom Drug Intermediates market can be classified into two types: GMP and Non-GMP. GMP, which stands for Good Manufacturing Practice, refers to the set of guidelines that ensure the production of high-quality pharmaceutical products, which are safe and effective for consumption. GMP-compliant intermediates meet stringent quality standards and are suitable for use in drug manufacturing. On the other hand, Non-GMP intermediates are not manufactured under such strict guidelines and may be used for research purposes or in industries where product quality regulations are not as stringent.</p></p>
