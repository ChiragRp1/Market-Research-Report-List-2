<p><p>Deaerator tanks are used in various industries to remove dissolved gases, such as oxygen and carbon dioxide, from feedwater before it enters a boiler system. Tray-type deaerator tanks feature multiple trays stacked inside the tank, which allow the water to come into contact with steam and eliminate gases. Spray-type deaerator tanks, on the other hand, use a series of sprays to disperse the feedwater and mix it with steam, effectively removing the gases. Lastly, there are other types of deaerator tanks that use different mechanisms to achieve the same gas removal process.</p></p>
