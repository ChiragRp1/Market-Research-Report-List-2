<p><p>Suprapubic catheters are medical devices inserted into the bladder through a small incision made in the lower abdomen. There are two types of suprapubic catheters: balloon and lumen. Balloon catheters have an inflatable balloon at the tip that helps secure the catheter inside the bladder. On the other hand, lumen catheters have multiple channels or lumens that allow drainage of urine and instillation of medication simultaneously. These different types cater to the diverse needs of patients requiring long-term bladder drainage or medication administration.</p></p>
