<p><strong>The Luxury Vinyl Flooring (LVT) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Dry-Back LVT</li><li>Loose-Lay LVT</li><li>SPC</li><li>WPC</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Luxury Vinyl Flooring (LVT) encompasses various market types, including Dry-Back LVT, Loose-Lay LVT, Stone Plastic Composite (SPC), and Wood Plastic Composite (WPC). Dry-Back LVT is adhered to the subfloor using an adhesive, providing durability and stability. Loose-Lay LVT is not glued down, relying on its weight and friction to stay in place. SPC is a rigid LVT type with a composite stone core, offering enhanced durability and dimensional stability. WPC features a composite wood core, providing a softer feel underfoot and increased resilience to moisture.</p></p>
