<p><p>A capsule checkweigher is a specialized equipment used in the pharmaceutical industry to accurately measure the weight of individual capsules. It is an essential tool to ensure quality control and compliance with industry regulations. The checkweigher weighs each capsule and compares it to a predefined weight range, flagging any capsules that fall outside of the acceptable range.</p><p>The market for capsule checkweighers has been growing steadily due to increasing demand for pharmaceutical products globally. The need for strict quality control measures and the growing emphasis on regulatory compliance have further fueled the market growth. Additionally, the rising adoption of automation in the pharmaceutical industry to improve efficiency and reduce errors has boosted the demand for advanced checkweighing equipment.</p><p>One of the latest trends in the capsule checkweigher market is the integration of advanced technologies such as artificial intelligence and machine learning.
