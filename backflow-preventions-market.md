<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>Backflow prevention market consists of various types of devices designed to prevent the reverse flow of potentially contaminated water into the public water supply. The Pressure Vacuum Breaker (PVB) utilizes a spring-loaded check valve to separate the potable and non-potable water. The Reduced Pressure Zone (RPZ) features two independently acting check valves and a relief valve to ensure maximum protection. The Double Check Assembly (DCA) incorporates two check valves to prevent backflow. The Atmospheric Vacuum Breaker (AVB) relies on air pressure to prevent backflow.</p></p>
