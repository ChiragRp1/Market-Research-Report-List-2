<p><strong>The Commercial Deep Fryer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Gas Commercial Deep Fryer</li><li>Electric Commercial Deep Fryer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The commercial deep fryer market is divided into two main types: gas and electric commercial deep fryers. Gas commercial deep fryers utilize natural gas or propane to generate heat, offering high cooking power and faster heat-up times. They are typically preferred for larger food establishments due to their high capacity and energy efficiency. On the other hand, electric commercial deep fryers operate using electricity and are commonly chosen for smaller businesses or establishments with limited ventilation. They provide consistent heat and are easier to operate, but may have slower heat-up times compared to gas fryers.</p></p>
