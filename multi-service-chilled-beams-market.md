<p><p>In the Multi Service Chilled Beams market, there are two main types: Bare Type and Mosaic. Bare Type refers to chilled beams that are designed for only one service, such as cooling or heating. These beams are simpler and more basic in their functionality. On the other hand, Mosaic chilled beams are designed to provide multiple services simultaneously, such as cooling, heating, ventilation, lighting, and acoustics. This type of chilled beam offers a more comprehensive and integrated solution for indoor climate control.</p></p>
