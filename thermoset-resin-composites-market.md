<p><p>The thermoset resin composites market includes two prominent types: Sheet Molding Compound (SMC) and Bulk Molding Compound (BMC). SMC is a composite material that involves a mixture of chopped fibers, fillers, and a thermoset resin. It is commonly used in applications that require high strength and dimensional stability. On the other hand, BMC is a dough-like material that consists of a blend of short fibers, fillers, and a thermoset resin. It is often utilized for molding complex shapes with intricate details due to its excellent flow characteristics.</p></p>
