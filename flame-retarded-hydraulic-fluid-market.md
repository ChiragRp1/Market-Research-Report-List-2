<p><p>Flame-retarded hydraulic fluids, such as HFA, HFB, HFC, and HFD, are used in various industries to ensure safety and minimize fire risks. HFA, or Water-in-Oil Emulsions, consist of a stable mixture of water and mineral oil. HFB, or Water-Polymer Solutions, are water-based hydraulic fluids containing polymers for flame resistance. HFC, or Water-Glycol Mixtures, combine water and glycol to provide fire resistance. HFD, or Synthetic Fluids, are non-aqueous hydraulic fluids that offer excellent fire resistance properties. These different types cater to a range of applications and environments, providing fire protection to hydraulic systems.</p></p>
