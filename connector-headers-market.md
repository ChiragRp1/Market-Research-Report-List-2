<p><p>The Connector Headers market comprises two main types: Double Connector Headers and Single Connector Headers. They are commonly used in applications that necessitate a high pin count. On the other hand, Single Connector Headers consist of a single row of connectors, providing a more compact and streamlined design. They are often utilized in applications that require fewer connections or limited space availability. Both types cater to diverse market segments and satisfy specific connectivity requirements.</p></p>
