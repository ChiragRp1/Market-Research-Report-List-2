<p><ul><li>Rapier (Single and Double Type)</li><li>Air Jet Loom</li><li>Water Jet Loom</li><li>Gripper or Projectile Loom</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The shuttleless loom market consists of several types of looms: rapier looms (single and double type), air jet looms, water jet looms, and gripper or projectile looms. Rapier looms use a rapier to transfer the weft thread through the shed, with single types having one rapier and double types having two. Air jet looms use compressed air to propel the weft thread, water jet looms use water jets, and gripper or projectile looms use grippers or projectiles to insert the weft thread. These different loom types cater to various textile manufacturing needs and offer different advantages in terms of speed, efficiency, and versatility.</p></p>
