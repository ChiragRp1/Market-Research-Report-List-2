<p><p>Feldspar is a versatile mineral used in various industries, with different market types. Orthoclase is a common type of feldspar that is rich in potassium and mainly used in ceramics and glass manufacturing. Plagioclase, on the other hand, contains a mix of sodium and calcium, and is preferred in the production of building materials and countertops. The "Others" category encompasses less common varieties, such as microcline and anorthite, which find applications in gemstone production and as ingredients in chemical industries. These distinct market types cater to diverse industries and consumer needs.</p></p>
