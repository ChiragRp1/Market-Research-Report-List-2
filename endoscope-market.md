<p><p>The endoscope market encompasses various types of devices used for medical procedures. Rigid endoscopes are traditional, non-flexible instruments that provide high-quality visuals but require larger incisions. Flexible endoscopes are more versatile, allowing access to difficult-to-reach areas through smaller incisions. Disposable endoscopes are designed for single-use, reducing infection risks. Capsule endoscopes are pill-sized devices that are swallowed and navigate through the digestive system to capture images without invasive procedures. These different types cater to diverse medical needs, optimizing visual clarity, accessibility, and reducing the risk of complications.</p></p>
