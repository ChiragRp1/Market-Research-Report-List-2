<p><p>Handheld portable projectors are categorized based on the technology used, such as DLP (Digital Light Processing) and LCOS (Liquid Crystal on Silicon). DLP projectors utilize microscopic mirrors to reflect light, resulting in sharp and vibrant images. LCOS projectors employ liquid crystals to produce high-resolution visuals with excellent color accuracy. Additionally, these projectors are classified based on physical resolution, including 480p, 576p, 720p, and others. Physical resolution determines the level of detail and clarity in the projected images. Understanding these market types helps consumers choose the most suitable handheld projector for their needs.</p></p>
