<p><p>The GMC-based motion controller is a system used in industrial automation to coordinate and control the movement of machines and equipment. There are three main market types for GMC-based motion controllers: PLC-based, PC-based, and stand-alone. PLC-based controllers integrate with programmable logic controllers to achieve precise and synchronized motion control. PC-based controllers utilize a computer system for advanced motion control applications and offer more flexibility and processing power. Stand-alone controllers are independent units that provide simple and straightforward motion control capabilities without the need for additional hardware or software integration.</p></p>
