Electro-acupuncture offers a more potent and controlled stimulation compared to traditional acupuncture, making it increasingly popular among patients and healthcare providers.</p><p>Additionally, technological advancements are driving the market growth. The development of portable and user-friendly electro-acupuncture units, which allow patients to receive therapy at home, has expanded the market's reach.
