<p><ul><li>Drop-on-demand</li><li>Continuous Feed</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Production inkjet web presses are high-speed printing machines used in large-scale commercial applications. Drop-on-demand inkjet presses enable precise control of ink droplets, ensuring accurate image reproduction. They release ink droplets when necessary, minimizing ink wastage. Continuous feed inkjet presses, on the other hand, use a continuous roll of paper, allowing uninterrupted printing without the need to reload sheets. These presses are commonly used for high-volume print jobs, such as newspapers or direct mail campaigns, where speed and efficiency are critical.</p></p>
