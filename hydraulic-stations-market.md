<p><p>Hydraulic Stations refer to systems that use pressurized fluid to generate power. There are four main types in the market. Swing hydraulic stations are utilized in heavy machinery and operate in a curved motion. Piston hydraulic stations use pistons to generate power and are commonly used in construction equipment. Plunger hydraulic stations, similar to piston stations, use plungers to generate power. Telescopic hydraulic stations have multiple stages that can extend or retract, making them ideal for applications like dump trucks. Each type has its own unique features and applications based on the specific needs of the industry.</p></p>
