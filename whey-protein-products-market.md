<p><strong>The Whey Protein Products Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Whey Protein Products Concentrate</li><li>Whey Protein Products Isolate</li><li>Whey Protein Products Hydrolysate</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The whey protein products market consists of three main types: whey protein concentrate, whey protein isolate, and whey protein hydrolysate. Whey protein concentrate is the least processed form, containing around 70-80% protein with some lactose and fat. Whey protein isolate undergoes further processing to remove most of the lactose and fat, resulting in a higher protein content of above 90%. Whey protein hydrolysate is enzymatically pre-digested, ensuring faster absorption and digestion. These different types cater to varying protein requirements and fitness goals of individuals.</p></p>
