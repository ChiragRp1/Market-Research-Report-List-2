<p><strong>The Uncooled Thermal Module Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Amorphous Silicon</li><li>Vanadium Oxide</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The uncooled thermal module market consists of two main types, namely amorphous silicon and vanadium oxide. Amorphous silicon modules utilize a thin layer of silicon, which absorbs infrared radiation and converts it into an electrical signal, allowing for temperature measurements. Vanadium oxide modules, on the other hand, rely on a material called vanadium oxide, which changes its electrical resistance depending on the temperature, enabling thermal imaging. Both types of modules are used in various applications such as surveillance, automotive, and industrial sectors for non-contact temperature measurements and thermal imaging purposes.</p></p>
