<p><p>Current Sensing Transformers are used in various industries to measure electrical currents. They come in three primary types: wound, toroidal, and bar. </p><p>Wound transformers consist of a primary winding and a secondary winding wrapped around a core. This design allows for accurate current measurement and insulation between windings. </p><p>Toroidal transformers feature a donut-shaped core with windings wrapped around it. They provide high accuracy, low power loss, and are compact in size. </p><p>Bar transformers have a secondary winding that encircles the primary winding. These transformers offer high linearity and are commonly used for precise current measurement.</p></p>
