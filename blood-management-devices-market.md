<p><p>Blood Management Devices Market includes various types of devices used in blood banks and healthcare facilities to manage and process blood samples. Blood grouping analyzers are used to determine a person's blood type. Blood warmers are used to regulate the temperature of blood prior to transfusion. Hematocrit centrifuges are used to measure the proportion of red blood cells in a blood sample.</p></p>
