<p><p>The Robot Steering Gear market includes two main types: Digital Steering Gear and Analog Steering Gear. Digital Steering Gear utilizes a digital control system to provide precise and accurate steering commands to a robot. This type offers advanced features such as programmability, high resolution, and improved control accuracy. On the other hand, Analog Steering Gear works based on an analog control system, which uses electrical signals to control the robot's steering. While analog steering gears may lack some of the advanced features of digital ones, they are generally more cost-effective and simpler to operate.</p></p>
