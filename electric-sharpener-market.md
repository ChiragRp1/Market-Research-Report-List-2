<p><p>The electric sharpener market offers two main types: single-hole and double-hole sharpeners. Single-hole sharpeners are designed to sharpen pencils of a specific size only. On the other hand, double-hole sharpeners have two openings, typically different in size, allowing users to sharpen both standard and jumbo-sized pencils. These versatile sharpeners are preferred by artists, architects, and anyone who uses different pencil sizes for their work.</p></p>
