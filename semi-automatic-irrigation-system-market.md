<p><p>The semi-automatic irrigation system market encompasses various types, namely sprinkler, drip, and surface irrigation systems. Sprinkler irrigation involves the use of overhead sprinklers to disperse water over a large area, mimicking natural rainfall. Drip irrigation involves providing water directly to the plant's root zone through a network of tubes and emitters, conserving water and minimizing evaporation. Surface irrigation involves flooding or furrowing the fields to distribute water across the surface. Each system type caters to different agricultural requirements, ensuring efficient water management for improved crop growth and productivity.</p></p>
