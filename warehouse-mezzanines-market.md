<p><p>Warehouse mezzanines are elevated platforms or floors that are constructed within a warehouse to maximize vertical space utilization. There are several types of warehouse mezzanines available in the market. Structural mezzanines are made from steel or aluminum and offer high load-bearing capacity. Catwalk mezzanines provide elevated walkways for personnel, allowing them to navigate between storage areas easily. Perimeter mezzanines are built along the walls of the warehouse, utilizing the available perimeter space. Other types of mezzanines may include freestanding, rack-supported, or multi-tier mezzanines, which are customized based on specific warehouse requirements.</p></p>
