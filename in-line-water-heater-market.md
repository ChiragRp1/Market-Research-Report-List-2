<p><p>An in-line water heater, also known as an instant or tankless water heater, is a device that heats water as it flows through the unit. Unlike traditional water heaters that store and heat a large volume of water, in-line water heaters heat the water only when it is needed, providing instant hot water. These systems are widely used in residential, commercial, and industrial applications due to their energy efficiency, space-saving design, and on-demand hot water delivery.</p><p>The global in-line water heater market has been experiencing significant growth in recent years and is expected to continue growing at a compound annual growth rate (CAGR) of 12.9% during the forecast period.
