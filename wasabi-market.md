<p><p>The wasabi market encompasses two main types: wasabi sauce and wasabi powder. Wasabi sauce is a condiment that is usually served with sushi or other Japanese dishes. It is made by blending grated wasabi root with other ingredients like soy sauce and mayonnaise to create a smooth, spicy sauce. On the other hand, wasabi powder is a dried and ground form of the wasabi root. It is commonly used for making wasabi paste by adding water, which is then served alongside sushi. Both these products cater to the demand for the distinct and fiery flavor of wasabi in different culinary contexts.</p></p>
