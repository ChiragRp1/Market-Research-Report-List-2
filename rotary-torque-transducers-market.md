<p><p>Rotary torque transducers are devices used to measure the torque or rotational force in various applications. Dynamic type transducers are designed for measuring torque in dynamic conditions, such as rotating machinery, where the torque changes over time. These transducers can accurately capture rapid changes in torque and are commonly used in automotive and aerospace industries. On the other hand, static type transducers are intended for measuring torque in stationary or slow-moving conditions. They are utilized in applications that require stable and precise torque measurements, such as quality control or calibration processes.</p></p>
