<p><p>Spray Polyurethane Foam (SPF) is a versatile insulation material that is commonly used in building and construction applications. When applied, SPF expands and solidifies, providing a seamless and highly effective insulation layer that can fill gaps and adhere to various surfaces.</p><p>One Component Foam (OCF) is a type of SPF that is available in pre-packaged cans. It is designed for smaller-scale applications and can be used for sealing gaps, cracks, and voids in walls, roofs, and other areas. OCF is simple to use, as it doesn't require any additional mixing or special equipment.</p><p>The future of the Spray Polyurethane Foam (SPF) and One Component Foam (OCF) market looks promising.
