<p><p>Ultrasonic probes are used for various medical and industrial applications to generate and receive ultrasonic waves. The market for these probes can be categorized into different types. Linear type probes consist of a straight transducer array and are widely used for imaging deep structures. Curved type probes have a curved array that allows for better visualization of organs such as the heart. Phased (Sector) type probes use multiple small elements to steer the beam electronically and are commonly used in cardiovascular imaging. Other types may include specialized probes for specific applications, such as intracavity probes or endocavity probes for internal examinations.</p></p>
