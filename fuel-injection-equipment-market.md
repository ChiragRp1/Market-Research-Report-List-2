<p><strong>The Fuel Injection Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Electronic Fuel Injection (EFI)</li><li>Mechanical Fuel Injection System (MFI)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Fuel Injection Equipment market consists of two main types: Electronic Fuel Injection (EFI) and Mechanical Fuel Injection System (MFI). EFI operates by electronically controlling the fuel injection process, enhancing fuel efficiency, and reducing emissions. It uses sensors to provide precise fuel delivery. On the other hand, MFI operates mechanically, utilizing a metering unit to control the fuel flow. MFI systems are generally simpler and more reliable but may not offer the same level of precision and control as EFI systems.</p></p>
