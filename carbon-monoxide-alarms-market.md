<p><p>The Carbon Monoxide Alarms market can be segmented into two types: Wall/Fixed Carbon Monoxide Alarms and Portable Carbon Monoxide Alarms. Wall/Fixed Carbon Monoxide Alarms are permanently installed on walls and are typically wired directly into a building's electrical system. They provide continuous monitoring and are suitable for residential and commercial applications. On the other hand, Portable Carbon Monoxide Alarms are compact and easily transportable, designed for use in various settings such as homes, offices, and vehicles.
