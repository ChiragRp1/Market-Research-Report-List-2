<p><p>The bike disc brake market is divided into two types: mechanical disc brakes and hydraulic disc brakes. Mechanical disc brakes work by using a cable to engage the brake pads, while hydraulic disc brakes use fluid pressure to activate the brake pads. Mechanical disc brakes are typically more affordable and easier to maintain, but hydraulic disc brakes offer better stopping power and more precise modulation. Both types have their own advantages and are suitable for different types of riders and terrain.</p></p>
