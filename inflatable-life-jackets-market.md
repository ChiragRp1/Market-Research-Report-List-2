<p><p>Inflatable life jackets are important safety equipment used in water activities. There are two main types of inflatable life jackets: vest type and yoke type. Vest type life jackets resemble a regular vest and are widely used in recreational boating and water sports. They provide buoyancy and support by covering the chest and back. Yoke type life jackets, on the other hand, have a collar or yoke that sits around the neck and shoulders and are commonly used in commercial shipping and offshore operations. They offer buoyancy and keep the wearer's head above water in case of an emergency.</p></p>
