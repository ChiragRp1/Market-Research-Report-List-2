<p><p>The Rigid Contact Lenses Market includes two main types: Rigid Gas Permeable (RGP) lenses and Orthokeratology (Ortho-K) lenses. RGP lenses are made from a hard plastic material that allows oxygen to pass through, providing a clear vision, while Ortho-K lenses are designed to reshape the cornea during sleep, temporarily eliminating the need for corrective lenses during the day. Both types offer advantages such as improved visual acuity and durability compared to soft contact lenses, but Ortho-K lenses have the additional benefit of potentially slowing down the progression of myopia.</p></p>
