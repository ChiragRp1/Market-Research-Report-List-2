<p><strong>The Anesthesia Machine for Animals Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Acyclic Loop</li><li>Loop</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The anesthesia machine for animals market is categorized into two types: Acyclic Loop and Loop market. Acyclic Loop refers to a system where the carrier gas is supplied directly to the patient without any recycling or rebreathing. In this type, exhaled gases are vented out and fresh gas is continuously delivered to the patient. On the other hand, Loop market refers to a system where exhaled gases are partially recycled and rebreathed by the patient. This allows for a more efficient use of anesthesia gases and reduces waste.</p></p>
