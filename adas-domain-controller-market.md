<p><p>ADAS (Advanced Driver Assistance Systems) Domain Controller refers to the hardware and software that enables various vehicle functions related to driver assistance. L2-L3 Domain Controller deals with automation up to Level 3, where the vehicle can perform some driving tasks under certain conditions. Domain Controller above L3 refers to a system capable of advanced automation beyond Level 3, allowing the vehicle to handle most driving tasks without driver input.</p></p>
