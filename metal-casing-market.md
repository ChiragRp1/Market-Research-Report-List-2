<p><p>The metal casing market is composed of different types of manufacturing processes that shape and produce metal casings for various applications. Stamping involves cutting and shaping metal sheets by using a die and a press, while die casting employs high pressure to inject molten metal into a mold. Extrusion pushes metal through a die to create consistent cross-sectional shapes. CNC (Computer Numerical Control) involves programming machines to precisely cut and shape metal casings. These techniques enable the production of metal casings with superior durability and accurate design specifications.</p></p>
