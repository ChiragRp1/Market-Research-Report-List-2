<p><p>Solar panel mounting structures are essential components for installing solar panels. There are various types available in the market. Roof-mounted structures are designed to be installed on the roof of buildings, making use of available space. Ground-mounted structures are installed on the ground and require a larger area. Top-of-pole mounted structures involve mounting panels on top of a pole, while side-of-pole structures attach panels to the side of a pole. Additionally, there are other mounting structures that cater to specific needs, such as those for water bodies or uneven terrain.</p></p>
