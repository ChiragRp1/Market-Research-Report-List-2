<p><p>Aviation Turbine Fuel (ATF) is categorized into two types: kerosene-type fuels and naphtha-type fuels. Kerosene-type fuels are widely used in aviation due to their high energy content and low volatility, making them suitable for jet engines. They have a lower flash point and exhibit better thermal stability. On the other hand, naphtha-type fuels have a higher flash point and are mainly used for supersonic aircraft and high-performance engines. These fuels possess high aromatics content, which provides improved combustion characteristics and enables higher engine efficiency.</p></p>
