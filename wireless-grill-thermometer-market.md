<p><p>The wireless grill thermometer market is comprised of two main types: Bluetooth and Wi-Fi. Bluetooth thermometers use short-range wireless technology to connect a thermometer probe to a mobile device, allowing users to monitor temperature remotely within a limited range. Wi-Fi thermometers, on the other hand, utilize Wi-Fi connectivity to provide a wider range of monitoring options. With Wi-Fi thermometers, users can access temperature updates and control features from anywhere through an internet connection. Both types offer convenience and flexibility in monitoring cooking temperature, but Wi-Fi thermometers provide greater accessibility and remote control capabilities.</p></p>
