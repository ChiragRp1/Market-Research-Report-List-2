<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>Chip On Film Underfill (COF) refers to a process of sealing electronic chips onto flexible films. The COF market consists of various types of underfill materials used for this process, including Capillary Underfill (CUF), where a liquid resin is drawn into the gap between the chip and film; No Flow Underfill (NUF) which cures without flowing to avoid damaging adjacent components; Non-Conductive Paste (NCP) Underfill, a low-cost option; Non-Conductive Film (NCF) Underfill, where a film is applied instead of a liquid; and Molded Underfill (MUF), where the chip is encapsulated with a solid molding compound for improved protection.</p></p>
