Single nozzle thermocompressors and multi-nozzle thermocompressors are two types available in the market. Single nozzle thermocompressors have one nozzle for steam condensation and are suitable for low-pressure applications. On the other hand, multi-nozzle thermocompressors have multiple nozzles for better efficiency and are commonly used in high-pressure systems. Both types play a crucial role in enhancing fluid flow and energy transfer in industrial processes.</p></p>
