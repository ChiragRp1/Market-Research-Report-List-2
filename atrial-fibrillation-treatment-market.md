Catheter ablation, which involves the use of radiofrequency or cryoenergy to destroy abnormal heart tissue, has shown promising results in restoring normal heart rhythm. It is considered a preferred treatment option for patients who are unresponsive to conventional medication. Furthermore, the development of anticoagulant drugs with enhanced efficacy and safety profiles is leading to improved management of atrial fibrillation and reduction in the risk of stroke.</p><p>Overall, the atrial fibrillation treatment market is projected to grow at a compound annual growth rate (CAGR) of 5.5% during the forecast period.
