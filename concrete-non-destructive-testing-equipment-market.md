<p><p>The concrete non-destructive testing equipment market consists of different types of equipment used for evaluating the quality and strength of concrete structures without causing any damage. Concrete test hammers are handheld instruments that measure the rebound of an impact to assess the concrete's strength. Ultrasonic pulse velocity testers use sound waves to determine the concrete's density and integrity. Pull-off adhesion testers measure the bond strength between concrete layers. Other equipment includes ground penetrating radar, impact echo testers, and infrared thermography, which are used for specific testing purposes.</p></p>
