<p><p>The industrial part washer market comprises two main types: solvent-based and water/aqueous-based. Solvent-based washers utilize chemicals, such as petroleum-based solvents, to clean industrial parts. These washers are known for their strong cleaning capabilities and ability to remove tough contaminants. On the other hand, water/aqueous-based washers rely on water as the main cleaning agent, often mixed with detergents or other additives. This type of washer is more environmentally friendly and typically used for parts that are less dirty or contaminated.</p></p>
