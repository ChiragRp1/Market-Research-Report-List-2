<p><p>The cordless pet clipper market can be divided into two types: variable speed and single speed. Variable speed clippers offer a range of speeds, allowing pet owners to adjust the blade's cutting power according to their pet's hair thickness or sensitivity. This versatility makes them suitable for various breeds and grooming needs. On the other hand, single speed clippers have a fixed cutting power that remains constant throughout use. While they may lack the adaptability of variable speed clippers, they are often more straightforward to operate and may be preferred for regular, standardized grooming sessions.</p></p>
