Thermal feedback systems monitor changes in skin temperature, helping users manage stress responses and improve relaxation techniques. Neurofeedback devices measure brainwave activity, helping individuals enhance focus, attention, and overall cognitive performance. Heart rate variability feedback tools analyze heart rate patterns, assisting individuals in managing stress and promoting emotional well-being. Electrodermal activity feedback devices monitor changes in the skin's electrical conductance, helping individuals monitor stress levels and enhance relaxation techniques.</p></p>
