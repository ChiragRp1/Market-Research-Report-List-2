<p><p>Super Clear Glass is a high-quality glass that offers exceptional clarity and transparency. It is available in two main market types: Rolled Glass and Float Glass. Rolled Glass is made by passing the molten glass through a pair of metal rollers, resulting in glass with a uniform thickness. Float Glass, on the other hand, is produced by floating the molten glass on a bed of molten tin, resulting in a glass sheet with a smooth and flat surface. Both market types cater to different applications and requirements in industries such as construction, automotive, and electronics.</p></p>
