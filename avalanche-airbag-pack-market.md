<p><p>The Avalanche Airbag Pack market includes two main types: Canister Based Airbag and Fan Based Airbag systems. Canister Based Airbags utilize a compressed gas canister that inflates the airbag when activated, typically through a pull cord. This system is reliable and has been widely used in the industry. On the other hand, Fan Based Airbags employ a fan to forcefully inflate the airbag using ambient air. This system offers the advantage of multiple deployments without the need for canister refills, but it may have limitations in extremely cold conditions.</p></p>
