<p><p>Sterile centrifuge tubes are commonly classified into three market types based on the shape of their base: conical base, round bottom base, and flat bottom base. Conical base tubes have a tapered shape which facilitates the separation of solids and liquids during centrifugation. Round bottom base tubes provide a larger surface area for the sediment or pellet to settle, making them ideal for cell culture and certain laboratory techniques. Flat bottom base tubes offer stability and are commonly used for storage and transportation purposes. These different types cater to diverse laboratory needs and applications.</p></p>
