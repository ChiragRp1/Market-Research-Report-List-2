<p><p>Glass tempering systems are categorized based on their operating mechanisms and capabilities. Horizontal systems are designed to temper glass sheets horizontally, providing high-quality and uniform results. Vertical systems, on the other hand, are specifically designed to temper glass vertically. Flat systems are capable of tempering flat glass sheets, while continuous systems enable a continuous production process. Oscillating systems oscillate the glass during the tempering process for enhanced strength. Bent systems are used for tempering curved or bent glass. Uni-directional systems temper glass in one direction, while bi-directional systems can temper glass in multiple directions.</p></p>
