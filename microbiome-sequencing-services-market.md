<p><strong>The Microbiome Sequencing Services Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Sequencing By Synthesis</li><li>Sequencing By Ligation</li><li>Sanger Sequencing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Microbiome sequencing services market types include sequencing by synthesis, sequencing by ligation, and Sanger sequencing. Sequencing by synthesis is a widely used method that relies on fluorescently labeled nucleotides to record the DNA sequence. Sequencing by ligation involves fragmenting DNA and ligating small oligonucleotides to generate a complementary strand. It is useful for identifying small variations in genetic sequences. Sanger sequencing is the traditional method where DNA is fragmented, amplified, and then subjected to capillary electrophoresis, allowing for DNA sequence determination.</p></p>
