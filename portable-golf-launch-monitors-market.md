<p><strong>The Portable Golf Launch Monitors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Personal-Grade Launch Monitor</li><li>Professional-Grade Launch Monitor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The portable golf launch monitors market includes two main types: personal-grade and professional-grade launch monitors. Personal-grade launch monitors are designed for casual golfers who want to analyze their game and improve their performance. These launch monitors are typically affordable, easy to use, and provide basic metrics such as distance, club speed, and ball speed. On the other hand, professional-grade launch monitors are more advanced and are primarily used by golf instructors, club fitters, and professional golfers. They offer more accurate and detailed data including launch angle, spin rate, workability, and shot dispersion. These launch monitors are usually more expensive but provide comprehensive analysis for golf professionals.</p></p>
