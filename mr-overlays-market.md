<p><p>MR Overlays, also known as Magnetic Resonance Overlays, are visual aids used in magnetic resonance imaging (MRI) to provide additional information to medical professionals. The increasing prevalence of chronic diseases and the growing demand for advanced diagnostic techniques are driving the market's growth. MRI is a non-invasive imaging technique that provides high-resolution images of the body, making it one of the preferred imaging modalities for various medical conditions. The demand for MR overlays is rising as they improve the diagnostic accuracy and aid in better treatment planning.</p><p>Moreover, technological advancements in MRI systems and imaging software are also contributing to market growth. The development of advanced visualization tools and software solutions has made it easier to create and integrate MR overlays with MRI scans. These advancements have not only improved the efficiency and accuracy of the overlays but have also reduced the overall time required for the analysis.</p><p>Furthermore, the growing adoption of artificial intelligence (AI) and machine learning (ML) in healthcare is opening new avenues for the MR overlays market.
