<p><p>Emergency exit lighting is an essential safety measure that helps people navigate their way out of a building during an emergency situation. There are three types of emergency exit lighting systems on the market. The self-contained power system operates independently and is suitable for smaller buildings. The central power system relies on a central battery or generator to power all the lights, making it ideal for larger buildings. The hybrid power system combines both self-contained and central power systems, offering flexibility and higher reliability for emergency lighting.</p></p>
