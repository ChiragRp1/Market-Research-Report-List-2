<p><p>The Airborne Wind Energy Equipment market can be categorized into two types: Aerostat and Autogiro. Aerostat systems utilize a large, lighter-than-air balloon that is tethered to the ground and equipped with wind turbines to capture the wind's energy at higher altitudes. Autogiro systems, on the other hand, employ a rotating wing that generates lift and connected to a ground-based generator via a tether. Both types aim to harness wind power efficiently and deliver it to the ground for electricity generation.</p></p>
