<p><ul><li>Single Input</li><li>Dual Input</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Benchtop Dissolved Oxygen Meters are devices used to measure the amount of oxygen dissolved in a liquid. Single Input meters have a single measuring channel and can measure oxygen levels in one sample at a time. These meters are compact and ideal for small-scale applications. On the other hand, Dual Input meters have multiple channels, allowing the simultaneous measurement of oxygen in two separate samples. Dual Input meters offer higher throughput and are commonly used in laboratories or industries where multiple samples need to be analyzed quickly and efficiently.</p></p>
