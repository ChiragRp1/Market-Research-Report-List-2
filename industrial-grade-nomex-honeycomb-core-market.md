<p><p>The industrial grade Nomex honeycomb core market consists of three main types of cells: hexagonal, rectangular, and pentagonal. Hexagonal cells are characterized by their six-sided shape, which provides high strength and rigidity. Rectangular cells are rectangular in shape and offer excellent shear and compressive strength. Pentagonal cells have five sides and offer a combination of high strength and lightweight properties. These different cell types cater to various industrial applications, allowing companies to choose the appropriate honeycomb core based on their specific requirements.</p></p>
