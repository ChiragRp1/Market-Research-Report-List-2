<p><p>The industrial refractory alumina market consists of different types of alumina, including calcined alumina, fused alumina, tabular alumina, and others. Calcined alumina is produced by heating aluminum hydroxide to remove moisture, resulting in a higher purity level. Fused alumina is formed by melting a mixture of alumina and other chemicals, creating a more durable material. Tabular alumina undergoes a high-temperature process to remove impurities, resulting in a high-strength substance. The "others" category includes various types of alumina with unique characteristics, serving different applications in the industrial refractory sector.</p></p>
