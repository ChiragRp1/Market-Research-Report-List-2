<p><p>Semiconductor encapsulation materials refer to substances used to protect and enclose semiconductors. The market is categorized into two types: epoxy based materials and non-epoxy based materials. Epoxy based materials are composed of epoxy resins and hardeners, offering good electrical insulation and mechanical protection. Non-epoxy based materials, on the other hand, utilize various compounds like silicones, polyurethanes, and ceramics, providing unique characteristics such as high thermal conductivity and excellent chemical resistance. Both types cater to the diverse needs of semiconductor encapsulation, ensuring the semiconductors' reliability and performance.</p></p>
