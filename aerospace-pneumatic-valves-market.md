<p><p>The aerospace pneumatic valves market can be classified into various types. Poppet valves are commonly used, featuring a reciprocating disk to control airflow. Pilot valves are smaller valves manipulated by a separate control source for regulating larger valves. Flapper-nozzle valves use a movable flapper to regulate airflow. Ball and plug valves utilize a rotating ball or plug for controlling flow. Lastly, other types of valves exist, providing different mechanisms for airflow regulation. These valve types play crucial roles in maintaining proper pneumatic control and functionality within the aerospace industry.</p></p>
