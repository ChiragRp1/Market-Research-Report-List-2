<p><p>IGCT (Integrated Gate-Commutated Thyristor) is a power device used in high-voltage applications. There are three main market types for IGCTs based on their characteristics. Asymmetric IGCTs feature different voltage ratings for forward and reverse blocking, allowing efficient power conversion. Reverse Blocking IGCTs can handle current flow in both directions, enabling bidirectional power flow control. Reverse Conducting IGCTs combine both functionalities, providing simultaneous forward and reverse current flow control. These market types cater to various applications in power electronics and allow for efficient control and conversion of electrical power.</p></p>
