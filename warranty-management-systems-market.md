<p><p>Warranty Management Systems (WMS) are software applications that help businesses manage warranty claims and processes. There are two main types of WMS market: On-Premise and Cloud. On-Premise WMS is installed on the company's own servers and managed internally. It provides complete control and customization options but requires significant upfront costs and maintenance. On the other hand, Cloud WMS is hosted on the provider's servers and accessed via the internet. It offers easy scalability, cost-efficiency, and flexibility, as updates and support are managed by the provider.</p></p>
