<p><p>The portable ultrasound system market comprises two primary types: trolley-based and handheld devices. Trolley-based ultrasound systems are larger in size and reside on a movable cart, offering higher processing power and advanced features. On the other hand, handheld ultrasound systems are smaller and more compact, facilitating easy mobility and point-of-care use. Both types have their own advantages based on the application and user requirements. Trolley-based systems are widely preferred in hospitals and clinics, while handheld systems are gaining popularity in emergency care, remote areas, and home healthcare settings.</p></p>
