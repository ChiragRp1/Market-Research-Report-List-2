<p><p>The Hydraulic Tyre Curing Press Market includes two main types: Dual Mode and Single Mode. Dual Mode presses are designed to cure both radial and bias tyres, making them more versatile. They provide high-pressure control, uniformity, and precision in the curing process. On the other hand, Single Mode presses are specially built for curing radial tyres only.
