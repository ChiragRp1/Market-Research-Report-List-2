<p><p>Acetic peracid, also known as peracetic acid or peroxyacetic acid, is an organic compound with the formula CH3CO3H. Acetic peracid is produced by the reaction of acetic acid with hydrogen peroxide.</p><p>The increasing demand for acetic peracid can be attributed to its versatility and effectiveness as a disinfectant and sterilizing agent, especially in the food and beverage industry. The growing concerns about foodborne illnesses and the need for superior disinfection solutions have significantly contributed to the market growth.</p><p>In the healthcare sector, acetic peracid finds applications in medical instrument sterilization, surface disinfection, and wound care products.
