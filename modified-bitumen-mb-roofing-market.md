<p><p>Modified Bitumen (MB) roofing is a popular choice for commercial and residential buildings due to its durability and longevity. There are two main types in the market: SBS (Styrene Butadiene Styrene) and APP (Atactic Polypropylene) type. SBS type is composed of rubberized polymers that provide flexibility and high resistance to temperature changes, making it suitable for areas with extreme weather conditions. On the other hand, APP type is made with plasticizers that improve its heat resistance, making it ideal for warmer climates. Both types offer excellent waterproofing capabilities and are easy to install, ensuring long-lasting protection for roofs.</p></p>
