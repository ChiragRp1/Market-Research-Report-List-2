<p><p>A staircase pressurisation system is a safety measure used in buildings to control the movement of smoke during a fire incident. This system helps maintain the pressure difference between the stairwell and adjacent areas, ensuring that smoke is prevented from entering the escape routes. It works by using fans and ducts to extract smoke from the stairwell, creating a pressure barrier that keeps the smoke confined to the affected area.</p><p>The global staircase pressurisation system market is expected to exhibit a significant growth rate during the forecast period. Factors driving this growth include the increasing focus on fire safety regulations and guidelines, the growing number of high-rise buildings, and the need for effective evacuation systems.
