<p><p>The traditional steamer is a type of food steamer that utilizes water and heat from an external source, such as a stovetop or a fire, to cook food. It consists of multiple layers or baskets where the food is placed for steaming. On the other hand, an electric steamer is a modern variation that operates using electricity. It usually has a single chamber with built-in heating elements, a timer, and temperature controls. Electric steamers are easier to use and offer more precise cooking options compared to traditional steamers.</p></p>
