<p><p>Household dough conditioners are ingredients used in baking to improve the quality and texture of dough. There are two types of dough conditioners available in the market: powders and fluids. Powdered dough conditioners are typically dry substances that are added to the dough mixture. They help in enhancing dough elasticity and volume.
