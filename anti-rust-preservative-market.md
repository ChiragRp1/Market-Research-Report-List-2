<p><strong>The Anti-Rust Preservative Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Oil Soluble</li><li>Water Soluble</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The anti-rust preservative market can be categorized into two types: oil soluble and water soluble. Oil soluble preservatives are formulated to mix easily with oil-based substances, making them ideal for protecting machinery parts that come into contact with oils and lubricants. They create a thin film on the metal surface, preventing moisture and oxygen from causing rust. On the other hand, water soluble preservatives are designed to mix with water, making them suitable for protecting metal surfaces that are regularly exposed to moisture or submerged in water. They form a protective barrier that prevents rust formation.</p></p>
