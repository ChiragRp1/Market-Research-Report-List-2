<p><ul><li>Sublimation Inkjet Printing</li><li>Direct to Garments Printing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Digital Textile Printer Market Types are Sublimation Inkjet Printing and Direct to Garments Printing. Sublimation Inkjet Printing utilizes a printer that transfers dye onto fabric using heat, resulting in vibrant and durable prints. It is commonly used in sportswear, flags, and interior decorations. Direct to Garments Printing, on the other hand, involves printing directly onto the textile using specialized printers and inks. This method enables detailed and high-quality designs on various garments, making it suitable for customized apparel, fashion, and promotional items.</p></p>
