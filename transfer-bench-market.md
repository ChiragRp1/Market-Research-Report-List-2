<p><p>Transfer benches are devices used in bathrooms to help individuals with mobility issues transfer safely into and out of bathtubs. There are two main types of transfer bench markets: retractable and ordinary. Retractable transfer benches are designed to be adjustable and easily folded away when not in use, offering convenient storage options for smaller bathrooms. On the other hand, ordinary transfer benches do not have a retractable feature and are typically more affordable.
