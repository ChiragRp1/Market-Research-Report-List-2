<p><p>Intravenous (IV) Iron Therapy is a medical procedure that involves the administration of iron compounds directly into the veins of patients with iron deficiency or anemia. IV iron therapy helps restore iron levels in the body, which is crucial for the production of hemoglobin and overall red blood cell function.</p><p>The global Intravenous (IV) Iron Therapy market is witnessing significant growth due to various factors. Firstly, the rising prevalence of iron deficiency and chronic kidney disease is driving the demand for IV iron therapy. Additionally, the increasing awareness among healthcare professionals about the benefits of IV iron therapy is contributing to the market growth. Moreover, the development of advanced iron compounds with improved safety and efficacy profiles is further fueling the market expansion.</p><p>The market is also witnessing several noteworthy trends. One such trend is the increased preference for intravenous iron therapy over blood transfusions, as it offers faster and more efficient iron replenishment. Iron Sucrose is a form of iron combined with sucrose, which is preferred for patients with renal problems.
