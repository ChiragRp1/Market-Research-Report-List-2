<p><p>Oxygen resuscitation equipment is used to administer oxygen to individuals experiencing respiratory distress. There are two main types of oxygen resuscitation equipment: self-inflating and flow-inflating. Self-inflating oxygen resuscitation equipment consists of a bag that automatically fills with oxygen when squeezed. This type is commonly used in emergency situations. Flow-inflating oxygen resuscitation equipment delivers a continuous flow of oxygen and is often used during surgeries or in intensive care units. Both types play a crucial role in providing respiratory support to patients in need.</p></p>
