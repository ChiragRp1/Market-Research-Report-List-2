<p><p>The MEMS package market comprises various types. LCCC, or Leadless Ceramic Chip Carrier Package, is a type of packaging where the chip is placed directly on a ceramic substrate without lead frames. MCM, or MulTI-Chip Module Package, involves multiple chips mounted together on a single package for enhanced performance. CSP, or Chip Size Package, refers to packaging where the size of the package is similar to that of the chip. Lastly, the "Others" category includes any other types of MEMS packages that may not fit into the mentioned categories.</p></p>
