<p><p>Meltblown PP Nonwoven fabric is a type of filter fabric made from polypropylene. The increasing demand for nonwoven fabrics in various industries such as healthcare, automotive, and construction is driving the growth of this market. The fabric's high filtration efficiency, lightweight, and low cost make it suitable for applications such as surgical masks, air filters, and protective clothing.</p><p>The COVID-19 pandemic has further accelerated the demand for meltblown PP nonwoven fabric, as it is a crucial material for manufacturing face masks and personal protective equipment.
