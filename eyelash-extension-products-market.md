<p><p>Eyelash extension products can be categorized into three types: vitamin-based, prostaglandin-derived, and peptide-based. Vitamin-based products contain ingredients that promote eyelash growth and strengthen the lashes. Prostaglandin-derived products stimulate the hair follicles and promote lash growth. These products may require a prescription as they contain a synthetic form of prostaglandin. Peptide-based products use peptides to enhance lash growth by nourishing and strengthening the lashes. Each of these product types offers different benefits for achieving longer and fuller eyelashes.</p></p>
