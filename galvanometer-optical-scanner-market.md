<p><p>Galvanometer optical scanner market comprises single axis and dual axis scanners. A single axis scanner deflects laser beams in one direction, allowing scanning in a one-dimensional field. On the other hand, a dual axis scanner can deflect laser beams in two directions, enabling scanning in a two-dimensional field. Single axis scanners are more commonly used in applications where scanning is needed in a specific plane, while dual axis scanners find application in tasks requiring scanning in multiple planes. Both types offer precise and efficient laser beam scanning solutions for a variety of industries.</p></p>
