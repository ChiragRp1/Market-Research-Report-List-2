<p><p>An emergency thermal blanket, also known as a space blanket or a Mylar blanket, is a lightweight sheet primarily designed to retain body heat and prevent hypothermia in emergency situations. They are widely used by outdoor enthusiasts, emergency responders, and aid organizations in various scenarios like search and rescue missions, disaster management, and survival kits.</p><p>The global emergency thermal blanket market has been experiencing steady growth over the years, driven by several factors. Firstly, the rising incidence of natural disasters and accidents resulting in increased demand for emergency response equipment has boosted the market growth.
