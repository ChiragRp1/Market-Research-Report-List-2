<p><p>The photographic lens market consists of two main types: prime lenses and zoom lenses. Prime lenses have a fixed focal length, meaning they offer a single magnification level. They are renowned for their superior image quality, wider maximum aperture, and compact design. On the other hand, zoom lenses have a variable focal length, permitting users to change the magnification level by adjusting the lens zoom ring. They offer versatility and convenience, as they cover a range of focal lengths, allowing photographers to capture a wider variety of shots without changing lenses.</p></p>
