<p><p>A Cardiac Holter Monitor is a portable device used to continuously monitor and record a patient's heart activity for a specified period, usually 24 to 48 hours. It is commonly used to diagnose and identify irregular heart rhythms, known as arrhythmias. The monitor records the electrical activity of the heart through electrodes attached to the patient's chest. The recorded data is then analyzed by a healthcare professional for any abnormalities or patterns.</p><p>The global Cardiac Holter Monitor market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 7.2% during the forecast period. <p><p>The Cardiac Holter Monitor market is classified based on the number of channels it supports for monitoring heart activity. In a 1-Channel monitor, only one electrical lead is attached to the patient's chest, providing limited information on heart functions. The 2-Channel monitor uses two leads, enhancing the data collected. Similarly, the 3-Channel monitor includes three leads for more accurate analysis. However, the 12-Channel monitor is the most advanced, utilizing twelve leads to comprehensively capture and monitor heart activity, enabling a detailed and precise examination of cardiovascular health.</p></p>
