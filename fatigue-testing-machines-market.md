<p><p>Fatigue testing machines are used to evaluate the durability and endurance of materials by subjecting them to repeated stress or strain. They are categorized based on the frequency range they can operate within. Under 30Hz machines are suitable for low-frequency applications, while 30-100Hz machines are generally used for medium-frequency testing. Machines falling in the 100-300Hz range are considered high-frequency equipment, capable of testing materials subjected to more intense and rapid stress cycles. Above 300Hz machines are designed for ultra-high-frequency testing, ideal for materials exposed to extreme and frequent stress conditions.</p></p>
