<p><p>Parabolic antenna market types include Electronically Controlled Antenna (ESA) and Mechanically Controlled Antenna (MSA). An Electronically Controlled Antenna (ESA) refers to a parabolic antenna system that can be electronically controlled to steer or direct the signal beam without any physical movement. This type of antenna offers high precision and flexibility in signal routing. On the other hand, a Mechanically Controlled Antenna (MSA) requires physical movement and manual adjustment to change the direction of the signal beam. While less flexible, MSA antennas are generally more affordable and widely used in various applications.</p></p>
