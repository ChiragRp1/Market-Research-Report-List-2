<p><p>Sleeve air springs, also known as bellows air springs or convoluted air springs, are pneumatic devices used in various industries for providing support and vibration isolation. These springs are made of a flexible sleeve made of rubber or other materials, which expands and contracts when air pressure is adjusted. They are commonly used in heavy trucks, buses, construction and mining equipment, and industrial machinery.</p><p>The future outlook for the sleeve air springs market is positive, with expected growth at a CAGR of 5.6% during the forecasted period. Additionally, the increasing adoption of air spring technology in industrial machinery and equipment is also contributing to market growth.</p><p>In terms of market trends, manufacturers are focusing on the development of advanced sleeve air springs with improved durability, performance, and reliability.
